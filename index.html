The Quiz

The quiz contains 15 questions and there is no time limit. There will be two types of questions, one is MCQ (Multiple Choice Questions) and the other is MSQ (Multiple Selected Questions).

There will be 10 questions for MCQ and 5 questions for MSQ. For MCQ you will get 1 point and for MSQ you will get 2 points.

At the end of the Quiz, your total score will be displayed. The maximum score is 20 points.

Start the Quiz. Good luck!
